2021-04-30: A hepatitis B virus causes chronic infections in equids worldwide

An HBV-related virus, which occurs globally in donkeys and zebras (Equid HBV, EqHBV). EqHBV presumably originated in Africa, approximately at the time when donkey domestication started. EqHBV causes a hepatotropic disease comparable to human HBV infection and prolonged infections, resembling chronic hepatitis B in humans.
